Strategic Dual Enrollment Pathway

Leverage CCP employee tuition remission benefits to earn an Associate Degree and Commonwealth Secondary School Diploma while homeschooling

🎯 Primary Goals

Associate Degree from CCP + Commonwealth Secondary School Diploma by age 18

✓ Zero tuition cost through employee benefits ✓ 2-4 year academic advantage ✓ Homeschool flexibility maintained

Option A: Intensive Accelerated
3 courses per semester • Summers free
  • Degree completed without summer courses
  • Maximizes employee benefit opportunity
  • Strong academic momentum
  • Summers free for family priorities

Considerations:

  • Higher semester workload needs excellent time management
  • Less flexibility during academic year
  • May challenge comprehensive homeschool approach
  • ACE credits count toward CSSD but only apply to degree if they fit requirements
Option B: Balanced Flexible
2 courses per semester • Strategic summers
  • Manageable workload for homeschool balance
  • Summer acceleration without overwhelm
  • Option for lighter 12th grade load
  • Maintains family time and flexibility

Considerations:

  • Requires summer courses for degree completion
  • ACE credits count toward CSSD but only apply to degree if they fit requirements
  • Need strategic planning for summer scheduling

Commonwealth Secondary School Diploma (CSSD)

Pennsylvania's state-recognized high school diploma equivalent through college credits

📅 Age Requirement

Minimum 18 years old at application

🎓 Credit Requirement

30+ college credits from accredited institution

📋 Status Requirement

Not enrolled in traditional high school

📜 Diploma Status

No existing high school diploma

✅ CSSD Benefits

Accepted for employment, military service, and higher education • No GED required • Demonstrates college-level achievement

Associate Degree Completion Process

⚠️ Important: Credits ≠ Automatic Degree

Simply completing 61+ credits does not automatically result in degree conferral. Students must:

  1. Declare a Major: Can only occur AFTER high school graduation
  2. Work with K-16 Office: CCP's K-16 office assists transition and may refer to academic counseling
  3. Credit Audit: Ensure completed courses satisfy specific degree requirements
  4. Apply for Graduation: Submit formal graduation application through registrar
