Associate Degree Completion Process

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Credits ‚â† Automatic Degree

Simply completing 61+ credits does not automatically result in degree conferral. Students must:

  1. Declare a Major: Can only occur AFTER high school graduation
  2. Work with K-16 Office: CCP's K-16 office assists transition and may refer to academic counseling
  3. Credit Audit: Ensure completed courses satisfy specific degree requirements
  4. Apply for Graduation: Submit formal graduation application through registrar
